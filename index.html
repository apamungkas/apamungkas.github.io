How the demos come together

Most labs here follow the same event-driven, cloud-native pattern — capture data at the edge, stream it, analyze in real time, and act automatically.

1

Capture

IoT devices and sensors read the physical world and emit lightweight JSON telemetry.

2

Stream

Azure Functions and Event Hub ingest events and pipe them into Microsoft Fabric.

3

Analyze

Fabric Eventhouse and Real-Time Dashboards turn raw streams into live insight.

4

Act

Activator triggers workflows and AI enriches alerts before delivering them to you.
